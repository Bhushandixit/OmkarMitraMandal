About Us

Welcome to Omkar Mitra Mandal

At Omkar mitra mandal, we are more than just a community; we are a vibrant celebration of Indian culture and tradition. Nestled in the heart of Kalewadi, Pune, our society is a testament to the rich tapestry of festivals and the spirit of giving that defines our heritage. We come together to commemorate joyous occasions like Ganpati, Navratri, Dahihandi, and many more, infusing our lives with the colors, music, and flavors that make our culture so unique. Beyond the festivities, we also hold the noble cause of food donation close to our hearts, striving to make a positive impact on our community. Join us on a journey where culture and compassion unite, and together, we'll create memories that will last a lifetime. Welcome to our vibrant, caring, and culturally rich family.
